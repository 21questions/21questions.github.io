About Us


Growing up, we've always cherished those moments spent around the table with friends and family, engaging in card games that effortlessly facilitated meaningful conversations. These games not only entertained us but also brought us closer, encouraging us to share stories, laughter, and even deeper insights.


As people who have experienced the incredible power of these card games firsthand, we've deeply passionate about creating an app that captures that magic. We believe in the ability of card games to ignite connections and enhance relationships, and we're dedicated to sharing this joy with others through our social card games app.


With every card drawn, we've personally witnessed the way these games can turn ordinary gatherings into unforgettable moments of connection. It's this profound impact on human relationships that drives us to provide a platform where friends, families, and couples can come together, just as we have, to enjoy the simple yet profound pleasure of card games that foster genuine conversations and stronger bonds.


Our mission is to bring friends, families, and couples closer together by providing a delightful social card games app that offers decks of engaging conversation starters. We aim to foster meaningful relationships, spark memorable discussions, and create lasting bonds among our users through enjoyable and enriching interactions.
